Meetup

Dear Cryo-ECS,

we as the Early Glaciologist Group (EGG) of the International Glaciological Society will be hosting the "EGG-Meetup", an online networking event on Zoom.
This will take place before the big summer break on July 13th, at 6PM UTC (8PM CEST)!

We will first give a little overview of what we are currently doing, before we will split up people into individual shuffled groups in breakout rooms, where we will have enough time for beers, chit-chat and scientific exchange 

Just sign up on the link below, put the meeting in your calendar and the get your beers ready!

Hope to see you soon on Zoom!
